About Us

Discover the story behind NutriSport+ and our mission to transform athletic performance

Fueling Potential Through Science

Founded in 2020, NutriSport+ emerged from a simple yet powerful idea: what if we could combine cutting-edge sports science, artificial intelligence, and personalized analytics to help every athlete reach their peak potential?

Our team of sports scientists, nutritionists, data analysts, and elite coaches work together to develop algorithms that adapt to your unique physiology, training regimen, and performance goals. We believe that every athlete deserves access to the same advanced tools previously available only to professional sports teams.

At NutriSport+, we're driven by our mission to democratize elite sports science and help athletes at every level Train Like Science Intended.

AI-Powered Performance Optimization

Combining machine learning with sports science for personalized training plans

Our Mission

To empower athletes of all levels with AI-driven insights that optimize nutrition, training, and recovery, making elite performance accessible to everyone.

Our Vision

A world where every athlete can achieve their full potential through personalized, science-based training and nutrition guidance.

Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal information

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, complete your profile, or use our services. This may include:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, date of birth)
  • Health and fitness data (height, weight, body metrics, training logs)
  • Nutrition information (dietary preferences, food logs, supplement use)
  • Device and usage information (IP address, browser type, app usage data)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Develop personalized training and nutrition plans
  • Process your transactions and send related information
  • Send technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All health and fitness data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. You can also object to or restrict certain processing of your data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@nutrisportplus.com.
